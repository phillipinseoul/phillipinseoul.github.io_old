About Me

I am an undergraduate student in School of Computing at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). I am currently working as an undergraduate research intern at 3D AI Lab, led by Prof. Minhyuk Sung.

My research interests are understanding and reconstructing the 3D structure of indoor scenes, and in turn generating novel views. Furthermore, I am interested in diverse tasks related to understanding the underlying 3D geometry from images, including Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) and 3D-aware generative models.
